Chromosome stats for A. thaliana

Use the dropdown to select the region of interest, and adjust the slider to look at the moving GC average across this region.

The smaller graph can be used to click and drag to investigate a region of interest in detail.

The graph was generated using fastaStats.py using all default settings on the concatenated chromosomal assembly of Arabidopsis thaliana. This whole genome took ~2 hours to compute on my laptop.
